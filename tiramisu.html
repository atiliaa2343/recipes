Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is my favorite dessert and it is the second recipie I decided to put on my first website. Tiramisu originated in Treviso in 1800. The dessert was invented by a clever "maitresse" of a house of pleasure in the centre of Treviso. The dessert was originally named "Tireme su" which means "pick me up". The dessert was developed as a natural Viagra in the 19th century,served to customers in a brotherl.

Ingredients

  • about 30 Savoiardi Ladyfingers
  • 2 1/4 cups of mascarpone cheese
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of Rum or Marsala
  • unsweetened cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Step 1:Make the coffee, then add 2 taplespoons or Rum or Marsala wine,but you don't have to.
  2. Step 2:Separate the egg whites from the yolks. Set aside the yolks and whip the egg whites until stiff.
  3. Step 3:Next, we beat the egg yolks with sugar for about 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Step 4:Pour the mascarpone cheese into a bowl and work it with a spoon to make it softer. Then add the yolks.
  5. Slowly whip the mascarpone cream for 2 to 3 minutes and then add the egg whites.
  6. Dip the ladyfingers quickly into the coffee and then arrange them into the pan.
  7. After putting the ladyfingers on the bottom of the pan, you can then spread the mascarpone cream over the ladyfingers.
  8. Add another layer of ladyfingers and then top with more mascarpone cream.
  9. Finally,sprinkle with cocoa powder. Allow to rest 3 hours in the refrigerator before serving.
