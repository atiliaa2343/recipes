Bread Pudding

Bread Pudding

Description

This is a popular recipe is used to make bread pudding. The recipe should take 45 minutes to make and it should make around 6 to 8 servings. Bread pudding originated in England around the 11th and 12th centuries. Bread pudding can be created in a bunch of different ways. Various types of fruit are put into bread pudding such as cranberries, oranges, raspberries, and apples. Bread pudding was invented as a way to prevent food waste. Cooks and homemakers would soak stale bread in a sweet liquid, baked the dish in the oven until it was golden and then served it warm.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Milk
  • 2 tablespoons (1/4 stick) Unsalted Butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • Pinch salt
  • 1/2 loaf sweet egg bread like challah or brioche, cut into 2 inch cubes(about 5 to 6 cups)
  • 2 eggs beaten

Steps

  1. Step 1: Heat oven to 350 degrees. In a pan heat up the warm milk,butter,vanila,sugar, and salt. Cook until butter is melted.
  2. Step 2: Butter up a 4 to 6 cup baking dish and fill it with cubed bread.
  3. Step 3: Add eggs to milk mixture and whisk; pour mixture over bread. Bake for 30 to 45 minutes. Serve warm.
